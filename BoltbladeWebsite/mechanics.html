Game mechanics

  • The game will have a 2D top-down view
  • Fast-paced, the knight will attack in a “hack and slash” style
  • The hero will also be able to perform a dash move, if you time it right you can use it to evade attacks
  • Special lightning abilities will have to be charged up using energy absorbed from enemies (perhaps energy orbs can drop randomly from enemies, or the energy bar can recharge based on attacks/damage made/kills). These abilities will be really powerful, able to kill several enemies at once
