Game mechanics

  • The game will have a 2D side-scroller view
  • Fast-paced, the knight's basic attacks will be fast melee attacks
  • It is recommended to always be moving in order to evade enemy attacks.
  • Special lightning attack. It will have a cooldown and an energy bar will indicate when it is ready. This attack will be really powerful, able to kill several enemies at once
